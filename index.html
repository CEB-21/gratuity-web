Privacy Policy

Overview

Gratuity is an iPhone tip calculator. The app is designed to work without creating an account, and most core calculations happen on your device.

Information we may collect

We may collect limited analytics and diagnostics information to understand app usage, improve features, and fix reliability issues. This can include events such as screen views, calculator interactions, settings changes, purchase flow events, and crash or performance data.

If you contact support, we also receive the information you choose to send by email.

What we do not require

We do not require you to create a Gratuity account to use the app. We do not sell your personal information. We do not use the app to serve third-party ads.

Receipt scanning

If you use the receipt scanner, the app uses your device camera and on-device text recognition to detect bill totals. Gratuity does not upload receipt images to our own servers as part of the normal scanner flow.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, the app stores supported settings and presets in your iCloud account through Apple services so they can sync across your devices. You can leave this feature turned off if you do not want to use it.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment details are handled by Apple, not by Gratuity directly.

Website analytics

This website uses Google Analytics through the Google tag shown on this page. That may collect standard website usage information such as page views, browser details, device information, and referral data.

How we use information

We use the information above to operate the app and website, improve features, monitor stability, understand which tools are helpful, and respond to support requests.

Sharing

We may use service providers such as Apple, Firebase, and Google Analytics to provide infrastructure, analytics, syncing, and app distribution. We do not sell your personal information to data brokers.

Your choices

You can choose whether to enable iCloud Sync, whether to contact support, and whether to continue using the website with analytics enabled in your browser. You can also stop using the app and remove it from your device at any time.

Contact
